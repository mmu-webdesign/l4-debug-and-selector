Disaster Area

Disaster Area is the name of the band in the series of Hitchhiker's Guide to the Galaxy Science Fiction books written by Douglas Adams .

Within the books they are generally regarded as not only the loudest rock band in the Galaxy, but also as being the loudest noise of any kind at all.
